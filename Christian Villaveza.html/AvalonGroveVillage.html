About


Welcome to Avalon Grove Village

Nestled amidst the picturesque landscapes, Avalon Grove Village is a charming and vibrant community like no other. Situated in a tranquil corner of the world, our village is a place where nature's beauty meets the warmth of a tight-knit community.

Our Story

Avalon Grove Village has a rich history dating back generations. Originally settled by pioneers seeking a peaceful haven, our village has grown and evolved over the years while preserving its timeless charm. We take pride in our heritage and continue to cherish the values of unity, harmony, and respect for the land.

The Essence of Avalon Grove

At Avalon Grove Village, we embrace the spirit of community living. Here, every neighbor is a friend, and every friend is family. Our tree-lined streets, vibrant parks, and community events create an atmosphere of togetherness that is truly unique.

Natural Beauty Abounds

Surrounded by lush forests, rolling hills, and crystal-clear streams, Avalon Grove Village offers a sanctuary for nature enthusiasts. Hiking trails, wildlife encounters, and stargazing are just a few of the experiences that await those who call this place home.

Join Our Community

We invite you to become a part of the Avalon Grove Village family. Explore our website to learn more about our community, upcoming events, real estate listings, and the incredible natural beauty that surrounds us.

Discover why Avalon Grove Village isn't just a place to live; it's a place to thrive, connect, and create lasting memories.

Avalon Grove Village Rules


• Trash and Recycling:

• Dispose of trash and recyclables in designated bins and follow the community recycling guidelines.


• Noise Control:

• Please keep noise levels to a minimum between 10:00 PM and 7:00 AM to respect the quiet hours.


• Pets and Animals:

• Pets are welcome but must be leashed when outside of your property


• Always clean up after your pets to maintain a clean environment.


• Property Maintenance:

• Maintain your property and landscaping to keep our village looking beautiful.


• Obtain approval from the homeowners' association for any major exterior changes.


• Respectful Behavior:

• Treat all residents and visitors with kindness and respect.

• Resolve disputes amicably and consider mediation if necessary.

• Pool and Recreational Facilities:

• Follow posted rules when using community pools and recreational areas.

• Supervise children at all times.
